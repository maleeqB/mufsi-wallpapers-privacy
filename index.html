Mufsi Wallpapers Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Feb 15, 2025

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data. Mufsi Wallpapers operates as a client-side application with no user accounts, tracking systems, or data collection mechanisms.

2. In-App Purchases

The app uses Google Play Billing for voluntary tips/donations through non-consumable in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Google Play Store. We never receive or store any payment information.

3. Permissions

The app requests:

  • INTERNET – For downloading wallpapers
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – For saving wallpapers (Android 10 and below)

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize:

  • Google Play Billing (payment processing)
  • Wallpaper sources: wallhaven.cc, reddit.com

5. Policy Changes

Updates will be posted at this URL. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
